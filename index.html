    WorkOut Tutorial

    You're Gym Partner!

    You might write down your schedule to solidify your commitment to your exercise plan. Working out with consistency is important for your physical health. A consistent workout regimen reduces stress and increases your mental well being. When you work out regularly, your body releases endorphins that enhance your mood.



    •               CHEST

      • "The chest muscles are responsible for moving the arms across the body and up and down, as well as other movements like flexion, adduction, and rotation."

      Dumbbell Squeeze Press

      Inclined Dumbell Bench Press

      Weighted Push Up

      Close-Grip Bench Press

    •           SHOULDER

      • "Important small muscles that surround the shoulder joint are called the rotator cuff muscles. They are the key muscles that keep the joint functioning properly. Weakness in any of the four muscles can cause instability, which can lead to numerous injuries."

      Barbell Shoulder Press

      Seated Dumbbell Front Raise

      Bent Over Dumbbell Raise

      Machine Lateral Raise

    •            ABDOMINAL

      • "Core strength exercises strengthen your core muscles, including your abdominal muscles, back muscles and the muscles around the pelvis. Strong core muscles make it easier to do many physical activities. You can do core strength exercises on a carpeted floor or mat."

      Two Point Bridge

      Back Extension

      Negative Crunch

      Twisting Back Extension

    •                 LEGS

      • "The more muscle mass stressed, the greater the release of these hormones. Because the legs, along with the glutes, contain some of the largest muscles in the body, heavy-resistance leg workouts do a lot to stimulate growth hormones and testosterone, which benefit muscle growth all over the body."

      Goblet Squats

      1/4 Squat Jumps

      Alternating Step Ups

      Mountain Climbers
